Breakdown of the Indian Constitution

The Preamble is an introductory statement that declares the purpose and objectives of the Constitution.

Fundamental Rights are basic human rights guaranteed to all citizens by the Constitution, ensuring liberty and equality.

Directive Principles are guidelines for the state to achieve social and economic justice.
